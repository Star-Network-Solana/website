The Trust Layer for Rust Education

Accreditation, verification, and open standards for credible Rust training.

Note: Built for ecosystems, institutions, trainers, and learners. STAR Network does not provide courses directly; it provides the standards and verification infrastructure.

Explore Standards View Trainer Registry

The Challenge

  • Rust training quality varies widely
  • Credentials are unclear or unverifiable
  • Organizers struggle to assess expertise

The Solution

  • STAR introduces a neutral trust layer
  • Makes trainer quality visible and verifiable
  • Reduces risk without lowering standards

What STAR Network Provides

Accreditation Standards

Clear criteria, rubrics, and review processes

Verified Trainer Registry

Searchable profiles with validated credentials

Learning Pathways

Structured, production-aligned skill journeys

Review & Improvement Loop

Outcome tracking and continuous refinement

How Trust Is Established

  • Open, publicly documented standards
  • Transparent governance principles
  • Neutral, ecosystem-aligned positioning
  • Ongoing review and updates

Who STAR Network Serves

Foundations & Program Owners

Reduce ecosystem and funding risk

Training Orgs & Communities

Upgrade credibility and structure

Trainers

Fair recognition, visibility, opportunity

Learners

Consistent, production-ready education

Rust adoption depends on high-quality education. STAR Network exists to ensure that those who teach are trusted, prepared, and aligned with real-world needs.
